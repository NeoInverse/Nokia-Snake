# рҹҚ– Gameplay Mechanics

## вӯҗ Small Food

* +5 score
* Increase snake length

---

## в—Ҷ Big Food

* Spawns every 10 foods
* Limited time only
* +15 score
* Extra growth bonus

---

## рҹҢҠ Wave System (Beach Map)

* Waves spawn randomly
* Hitting wave:

  * reduces score
  * cuts snake length

---

## рҹ’Ј Bomb System (Stone Chamber)

* Bombs spawn after several foods
* Explode after timer
* Blast radius damage
